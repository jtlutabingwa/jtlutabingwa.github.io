Website Evaluations

Apple Evaluation

Apple's official website shows its products and services with a simple design, emphasizing high-quality visuals and easy website navigation. Apple sells a variety of technology and services for users.

  • Contrast: Effective use of white space, bold images, and a clear print that makes the website more enhances readable.
  • Repetition: Consistent colors and layout ensure an easy user experience.
  • Alignment: Clean and structured layout provides easy and simple navigation.
  • Proximity: Related elements are put together in a clear way, improving access to information.

Apples website is what other websites should strive to be in overall design and readability. They showcase easy reaability and site navigation that most people could do with no problem. Apple meets all of the CRAP principles with no challenge.

Craigslist Evaluation

Craigslist is an ads website that, has an outdated design that makes usability challenging. It provides users wuth the ability to purchase a variety of different belongings and assets that range low prices to high prices.

  • Contrast: Minimal contrast with a basic color scheme, making it visually unappealing.
  • Repetition: Cnsistent but, the design is very simplistic and lacks engaging elements.
  • Alignment: Content is aligned in a grid, but the cluttered nature of links and text makes it hard to read.
  • Proximity: Poor spacing between sections can make it hard to find different categories of information.

Craiglist's website is very overwhelming when your first click on the website. Being shown with a very cluttered website with a variety of links that hard to read. Though the website is simple it could definitley imporve uin areas such as fonts, readbability, links, and etc.

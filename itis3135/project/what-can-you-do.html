All on you

You can make a difference by choosing second-hand clothing, repairing what you own, participating in clothing swaps, and supporting local sustainable brands. Even small actions add up to a big impact!

  • Donate clothes instead of tossing them
  • Use eco-friendly detergents
  • Shop locally or from verified sustainable brands

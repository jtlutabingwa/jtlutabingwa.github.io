Joyful Lion Traveling Bookstore

The Joyful Lion Traveling Bookstore is a bookstore on wheels with the mission of bringing joy to kids and adults who love the adventure of a good book. We offer books from all genres and host storytime sessions for children who enjoy listening to engaging stories read by our employees. In addition to books, our traveling bookstore features a creative corner where visitors can unleash their imagination and enjoy arts and crafts. Founded in 2020 by Jonathan Lutabingwa, The Joyful Lion Traveling Bookstore was born out of his passion for books. He launched his first traveling bookstore from a renovated old bus and has since expanded his fleet. His goal is to continue inspiring people to read and embark on countless literary adventures with the books he offers.
